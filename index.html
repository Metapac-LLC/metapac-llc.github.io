What are we about?

We are an independent-expenditure only committee focused on improving education and research involving election law, specifially surrounding SuperPACs





Why Election Finance Law?

So little is know about election law in general, especially so in the public. It's no secret that money is excedingly influential in our political process. It is necessary to understand how its flow is managed in political circles and how that effects policy.

Why Now?

Recent foreign involvement in our political process has show how cheaply elections can be influenced. The time is now to understand the law surrounding election finance more thuroughly, using these recent events as a point of comparisson.

Why is Education on Election Finance Law Necessary?

It is impossible for our democracy to function properly without widespread knowlage of how all of the media that they consume is being paid for. Furthermore, the democritization of knowlage on how to leverage the legal framework inplace will allow the public to utilize it more freely.
